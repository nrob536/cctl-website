Welcome to the Toy Library

Welcome to your community toy library! We offer a wide range of toys for children of all ages, available for short-term borrowing.

How It Works

  1. Browse our toy catalogue to see what's available
  2. Book a toy by entering your user ID (no password needed)
  3. Pick up your toy and enjoy it for 14 days
  4. Renew your booking up to 2 times if you need more time
  5. Return your toy when you're done
